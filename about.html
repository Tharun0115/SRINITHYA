Who We Are

Srinithya Engineering Private Limited (SEPL) was established with a singular vision: to revolutionize the construction infrastructure sector in India. We recognized a gap in the market for machinery that is not only technologically advanced but also rugged enough to withstand the demanding conditions of Indian construction sites.

Today, we stand as a dual-force entity: a direct manufacturer of heavy-duty Bar Processing Machinery and a premier trading house for global Light Construction Equipment. By combining indigenous engineering with international standards, we deliver solutions that reduce downtime and maximize productivity.
