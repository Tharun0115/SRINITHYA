Vibrator Models

Electric & petrol vibrators for concrete consolidation.

Compatibility Note

These vibrators are compatible with our range of Mechanical Needles.

3HP 1PH Electrical Vibrators

Single-phase 3HP motor with high centrifugal force for medium-sized pours.

  • Power Output: 3 HP (approx. 2.2 kW)
  • Voltage: 220V – 240V AC
  • Current (Amps): 12 – 15 A
  • Frequency: 50 / 60 Hz
  • Insulation Class: Class F (155°C)
  • Speed (RPM): 2800 – 3000 RPM

3HP 3PH Electrical Vibrators

Three-phase 3HP for continuous heavy-duty consolidation on large pours.

  • Power Output: 3 HP (approx. 2.2 kW)
  • Voltage: 380V – 415V AC
  • Current (Amps): 4.5 – 6 A
  • Frequency: 50 / 60 Hz
  • Insulation Class: Class F (155°C)
  • Speed (RPM): 2850 – 3000 RPM

2HP Electrical Vibrators

Compact 2HP units ideal for small pours and repair works.

  • Power Output: 2 HP (approx. 1.5 kW)
  • Voltage: 220V – 240V AC
  • Current (Amps): ~8 – 10 A
  • Frequency: 50 / 60 Hz
  • Insulation Class: Class F (155°C)
  • Speed (RPM): 2800 – 3000 RPM

Diesel Vibrator

Robust diesel-powered vibrator for heavy-duty site applications.

  • Power Output: 5 HP
  • Fuel Type: Diesel
  • Speed (RPM): 3000 RPM
  • Mount: Heavy Duty Frame

Petrol Vibrator

Versatile petrol-powered vibrator for sites without electricity.

  • Power Output: 5.5 HP
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Speed (RPM): 3000 RPM
  • Mount: Heavy Duty Frame
