Concrete Mixer Models Catalogue

Portable mixers ranging from 220L (1 bag) to 350L (2 bag) capacity.

CM-220L (Electric)

Standard 1-bag mixer ideal for small jobs and indoor sites with electrical access.

  • Capacity: 220 Litres
  • Power: 3 HP Electric Motor
  • Mobility: Towable on site

CM-220L (Engine Powered)

Heavy-duty mixer for remote construction sites without reliable electricity.

  • Capacity: 220 Litres
  • Power: 5 HP Diesel Engine

CM-350L (Electric)

  • Capacity: 350 Litres
  • Power: 5 HP Electric Motor
  • Mobility: Heavy Duty Chassis

CM-350L (Engine Powered)

Maximum capacity mixer with powerful engine for continuous, high-volume outdoor work.

  • Capacity: 350 Litres
  • Power: 9 HP Diesel Engine
  • Mobility: Heavy Duty Chassis
